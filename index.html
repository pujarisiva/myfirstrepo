About Kia Motors

Kia Corporation, founded in 1944, is South Korea's second-largest automobile manufacturer. With a vision to create sustainable mobility solutions, Kia has become a global leader in automotive innovation.

Kia's philosophy of "Movement that inspires" reflects our commitment to creating vehicles that not only transport people but also inspire them through design, performance, and technology.

With a presence in over 190 countries, Kia continues to push boundaries with advanced safety features, eco-friendly technologies, and customer-centric services that redefine the driving experience.

Kia India Manufacturing Plant

Kia's state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Penukonda, Andhra Pradesh, represents one of the most advanced automotive plants in India. Spread over 536 acres, this facility has an annual production capacity of 300,000 vehicles.

The plant incorporates Industry 4.0 technologies, including over 450 robots and advanced automation systems, ensuring precision manufacturing and consistent quality across all Kia models produced in India.

Kia's commitment to 'Make in India' is evident through this facility, which not only serves the domestic market but also exports vehicles to over 70 countries worldwide, strengthening India's position as a global automotive manufacturing hub.
